About FocusFlow Lite

Simple productivity for busy people

Our Mission

FocusFlow Lite was created to solve a simple problem: most productivity apps are too complicated. We believe that planning your day should take minutes, not hours.

What Makes Us Different

While other apps add more features, we focus on removing friction. Our goal is to help you spend less time organizing and more time doing.

The Team

FocusFlow Lite is developed by a small team of designers and developers who understand the importance of keeping things simple. We use our own product every day.
